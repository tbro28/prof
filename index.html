Intro

I began my career on the test side of software development and have had some connection to testing ever since. Starting out primarily in a functional black-box test position, I have moved through a variety of roles involving automation, network performance engineering, and supporting large scale infrastructure procedures for high availability systems. Current responsibilities include supporting the deployment of cloud-based architectures, in conjunction with the integration of tools to allow for continuous integration and deployment.

I have had opportunities to work on numerous projects involving internally developed systems, along with well-known external systems. Most projects have had distributed architectures in support of a global customer base with more recent ones involving cloud infrastructure utilization. I finished my second master’s degree, an MBA, in August of 2014 and recently finished a certification in cloud deployment/operations in AWS.

Looking for ways to use new technologies to help drive success always captures my imagination. I’ve been lucky enough to have moved around within Boeing several times to work on projects that are implementing ideas and products that push past previous limits to open up new ways of doing business. That to me is exciting; not simply being told a future vision, but to be actually working with concepts and technologies that expand the possibilities.
